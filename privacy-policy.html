Privacy Policy for Watched

  • Movie Data: Movies you've watched, rated, and added to watchlists

Device Information

We use the collected information to:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these cases:

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest

  • Secure authentication systems

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the personal information we have about you

  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Control Sharing: Manage privacy settings for social features

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:
