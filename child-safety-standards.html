Child Safety Standards - Watched App

Effective Date: January 3, 2025

Last Updated: January 3, 2025

Watched is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users, with zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

Watched takes child safety extremely seriously and maintains strict policies to prevent, detect, and respond to any content or behavior that could harm children.

Zero Tolerance Policy:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Absolutely prohibited
  • Child Exploitation: Any form is strictly forbidden
  • Inappropriate Contact: Adults targeting minors for inappropriate purposes
  • Harmful Content: Content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers children

2. Prevention Measures

Content Moderation:

  • Automated Detection: Systems to identify potentially harmful content
  • User Reporting: Easy reporting mechanisms for concerning content
  • Manual Review: Human moderation of flagged content
  • Immediate Removal: Swift action on confirmed violations

User Verification:

  • Account Authentication: Email verification required
  • Age Verification: Users must confirm they are 13+ years old
  • Profile Monitoring: Regular review of user profiles and activity

3. Detection and Response

Reporting System:

  • 24/7 Reporting: Users can report concerning content anytime
  • Anonymous Reporting: Option to report without revealing identity
  • Priority Handling: Child safety reports receive immediate attention
  • Professional Review: Trained moderators handle all reports

Response Actions:

  • Immediate Suspension: Accounts involved in CSAE are immediately suspended
  • Content Removal: Harmful content is removed within 1 hour of confirmation
  • Law Enforcement: We cooperate fully with authorities on illegal content
  • Permanent Bans: Zero tolerance means permanent removal for violations

4. Age-Appropriate Design

  • 13+ Age Restriction: App designed for users 13 years and older
  • Privacy by Design: Strong privacy protections for younger users
  • Limited Data Collection: Minimal data collection from users under 18
  • Parental Controls: Tools for parents to monitor younger users

5. Staff Training and Compliance

  • Regular Training: All staff receive child safety training
  • Background Checks: Comprehensive screening for all team members
  • Compliance Monitoring: Regular audits of safety procedures
  • Industry Standards: Adherence to best practices and legal requirements

6. Technology and Partnerships

  • PhotoDNA Technology: Microsoft's technology for detecting known CSAM
  • Industry Collaboration: Partnerships with safety organizations
  • NCMEC Reporting: Reports to National Center for Missing & Exploited Children
  • Regular Updates: Continuous improvement of safety measures

7. Legal Compliance

Watched complies with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including:

  • Child Protection laws in all operating jurisdictions
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • GDPR Article 8 (Child Consent)
  • Regional and national child safety regulations

8. Reporting and Contact Information

To report child safety concerns:

  • Emergency: Contact local law enforcement immediately
  • CSAM Reports: safety@watched-app.com
  • General Safety: support@watched-app.com
  • Phone: Available 24/7 for urgent safety concerns

Response Time: Child safety reports are reviewed within 1 hour, 24/7.

9. Transparency and Accountability

  • Regular Reports: Quarterly safety transparency reports
  • Third-party Audits: Independent safety assessments
  • User Education: Safety resources and guidelines
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular policy updates based on best practices

10. Updates to This Policy

This Child Safety Standards document is reviewed and updated regularly to ensure:

  • Compliance with evolving legal requirements
  • Implementation of industry best practices
  • Response to new threats and technologies
  • Incorporation of user and expert feedback

Users will be notified of significant changes via in-app notifications and email.

Watched is committed to creating the safest possible environment for all users, especially children. We take this responsibility seriously and appreciate the trust placed in us by our community.

For questions about this policy: legal@watched-app.com
